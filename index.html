HOW TO PLAY

Swipe your finger on the tile grid to form words

Enter a valid word within 60 seconds to reset the timer

Longer words earn more points

The same tile can be used multiple times in a word

Used letters are replaced by letters at the top

One 2-tile swap can be preformed per game

Hard Mode: 30 Second timer
