How to play

  • rearrange the grid to hunt for high scoring words
  • a perfect hunt results in exactly 1000 total points
  • end the game to submit score to the leaderboard

Gameplay

  • played letters are replaced by upcoming letters
  • hunt until all 50 replacement letters are used up
